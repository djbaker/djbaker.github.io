Gmina Brochów is a rural gmina (administrative district) in Sochaczew County, Masovian Voivodeship, in east-central Poland. Its seat is the village of Brochów, which lies approximately 11 kilometres (7 mi) north of Sochaczew and 52 km (32 mi) west of Warsaw. The gmina covers an area of 116.76 square kilometres (45.1 sq mi), and as of 2006 its total population is 4,258.
